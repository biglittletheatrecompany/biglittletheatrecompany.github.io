The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-time


The Big Little Theatre Company returns in April with a bold and visually inventive production of The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time. Renowned for producing outstanding young performers and theatre of exceptional quality, BLTC Inc. brings Mark Haddon’s much-loved story to life through dynamic ensemble work, imaginative staging, and powerful storytelling. Following the extraordinary journey of Christopher - a teenager who sees the world differently - this moving and funny production explores mystery, family, and courage with warmth and originality. About Big Little Theatre Company:

The Big Little Theatre Company is a two-pronged Ashburton-based theatre company.

BLTC inc, is a production company which has produced well over 15 separate shows, from pantomime to Shakespeare. They have performed on The Court Theatre Stage, trained at The Globe Theatre in London, The Egg Theatre in Bath and in Stratford upon Avon with The Royal Shakespeare Company. The company's aim is quite simple – to enable all students to stand strong in their own space and to love the theatre, its power and magic for ever.

BLTC actor training meets weekly and trains young actors (aged 6 – 18) in all aspects of stagecraft, preparing them for life as well as examination and performance. We train every Tuesday and offer a unique series of acting workshops. We also prepare students for Trinity College (London) Examinations, and have a track record of outstanding success.
