Coming Up:

Don’t miss our (Covid-delayed) ANZAC show, finally hitting the stage in August: ‘There’s an Element of Risk’. The show is set in 1946, where a motley cast from the local village are rehearsing a show to cheer the spirits on the ‘Home Front’. The show follows the cast’s rehearsals, taking the audience with them into the hearts and minds of those who served, those who stayed behind, those who loved and those who lost. The last BLTC production sold out for every show, so plan ahead and secure your tickets soon. Tickets will be available here soon for the following shows:
Friday 19 August 7pm: Mt Somers Hall.
Saturday 20 August 7pm: Halswell Community Hall.
Sunday 28 August 2pm: Ashburton Events Centre.
